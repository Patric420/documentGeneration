Mr. \textbf{{{Contractor_Name}}}, S/o \textbf{{{Contractor_Father_Name}}} R/o \textbf{{{Contractor_Address}}} having Aadhaar No. \textbf{{{Contractor_Aadhaar}}} (hereinafter referred to as the ``Independent Contractor'' which expression shall wherever the context, mean and include his Affiliates, successors, representatives and assigns).

\vspace{1em}
\noindent
The Company and the Independent Contractor are hereinafter individually referred to as the ``Party'' and collectively as the ``Parties''.

\vspace{1.5em}
\noindent\textbf{WHEREAS:}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
A. The Company is engaged in the business of writing, modifying, testing, developing, designing, implementing, customizing, installing, maintaining, upgrading, supporting, and servicing computer programs and software applications to meet the specific needs of particular clients, excluding web-page designing and other ancillary work.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
B. The Independent Contractor has expressed his readiness and willingness to work for the Company.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
C. On basis of express representation, the Company wishes to engage the Independent Contractor as \textbf{{{Role}}}.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
D. The Parties have, thus, decided to reduce the terms of their understanding into writing.

\vspace{1em}
\noindent
NOW THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual promises and covenants contained herein, the Parties agree as follows: -

\vspace{1em}
\noindent
\textbf{1. Engagement and Services}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{1.1 Engagement.} The Company hereby engages the Independent Contractor to provide service as assigned to him by the Company from time to time, and the Consultant hereby accepts the engagement.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{1.2 No Employer-Employee relationship.} It is understood and agreed between the parties that the Independent Contractor shall not be an employee of the Company. It is further agreed that the Independent Contractor shall not be entitled to any rights or benefits of a permanent employee.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{1.3 Designation.} The Independent Contractor, however, shall be allowed to use the designation of `\textbf{{{Role}}}' while interacting with third parties in relation to the Services.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{1.4 Standard of Services.} The Company shall expect the Services to be performed with promptness, diligence and at a level of proficiency expected of an Independent Contractor with similar experience and exposure.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{1.5 Providing Information.} The Company shall provide such access to its information, property and personnel as the Independent Contractor may reasonably require to enable and facilitate him to perform the Services.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{1.6 Ethical Standards.} The Independent Contractor shall adhere to highest ethical standards and shall not indulge in any unlawful or corrupt practises.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{1.7 Representations and Warranties.} The Independent Contractor represents and warrants to the Company that he is under no contractual or other restrictions or obligations inconsistent with the execution of this Agreement or interfering with the performance of the services hereunder.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{1.8 Non-compete and Confidentiality Agreement.} The Independent Contractor shall execute and comply with the Non-Compete and Confidentiality Agreement and any other agreement(s), as may be required by the Company.

\vspace{1em}
\noindent
\textbf{2. Effective Date, Duration, Termination}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{2.1 Commencement.} This Agreement shall commence with effect from the execution hereof and shall remain in effect for a period of {{Duration}} therefrom, unless terminated earlier as provided in Clause 2.2.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{2.2 Termination.} This Agreement may be terminated by either Party, without cause and without liability, by giving {{Notice_Period}} prior written notice.
\newline
In addition, the Company shall also have the right to terminate this Agreement of the Independent Contractor with immediate effect in the event of material breach of any recital.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{2.3 Effect of Termination.} Upon termination of this Agreement, all legal obligations, right and duties of the Parties arising out of this Agreement shall terminate except for those as shall have accrued prior to termination and except as otherwise expressly provided in this Agreement.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{2.4 Renewal or Extension.} Upon expiry of this Agreement, the Parties may renew this Agreement for such duration and on such terms and conditions, as may be mutually agreed. However, there shall be no obligation upon any Party for such renewal.

\vspace{1em}
\noindent
\textbf{3. Fee and Expenses}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{3.1 Fee.} In lieu of services provided to the Company, the Independent Contractor shall be paid Rs. {{Contractor_Fee}}/- per month as ``Contractor Fee''.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{3.2 Reimbursement of Expense.} Additionally, the Independent Contractor shall be entitled to reimbursement for all pre-approved expenses incurred in performance of the Services, upon submission and approval of receipts given as evidence of such expenses.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{3.3 Payment.} The Independent Contractor shall be paid after deduction of applicable taxes, if any, applicable on the transaction.

\vspace{1em}
\noindent
\textbf{4. Intellectual Property and License}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{4.1 Definition.} In this Agreement, the term ``Intellectual Property'' shall include any ideas, concepts, creations, discoveries, inventions, know how, trade secrets; trademarks, service marks, designs, utility models, tools, devices, models, methods, procedures, processes, systems, principles, algorithms, works of authorship, flowcharts, drawings, and other confidential and proprietary information, computer programming code, databases, software programs including their source code; data documents, instruction manuals, policies, promotional and advertising material, other literature, documentation and materials, records, memoranda, notes, user guides; in printed or machine-readable form, which are in existence at the date of this Agreement or which may come into existence thereafter, any ideas or concepts, reports, recommendations, analysis, press materials, speeches, white papers and other information, data, and materials developed or prepared by Independent Contractor either singly or jointly with Company officials, in connection with his engagement with the Company.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{4.2 Ownership.} The Independent Contractor irrevocably, absolutely and perpetually assigns to the Company worldwide rights in respect of all his right/titles, and interests under any statute or common law including patent rights, copyrights including moral rights, trademarks, designs, anywhere in the world, whether negotiable or not in respect of his contributions free from encumbrances of any kind for the full term of each and every such right, including renewal or extension of any such term.
\newline
All of the Intellectual Property (created solely by the Independent Contractor or in collaboration with team members or third party whether or not connected with the Company) created, developed or prepared in connection with his engagement with the Company shall be deemed to be works and/or inventions developed in the course of his engagement with the Company within the meaning of copyright laws, and the Company shall be deemed to be the sole author and owner thereof of the said Intellectual Property in all jurisdiction in India and across the globe.
\newline
In under any applicable law, any result and proceeds of the services of the Independent Contractor during the term of engagement with the Company are not deemed to be the work and or invention developed in the course of the engagement, then the Independent Contractor hereby agrees to assign and transfer the Company all the rights, titles and interest in such works and inventions unconditionally and fully, without any restriction whatsoever.

\vspace{1em}
\noindent
\textbf{5. Confidential Information}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{5.1 Exposure to Confidential Information.} In course of engagement, the Independent Contractor shall be exposed to Confidential information about the business of the Company, its clients which (i) amounts to a trade secret; (ii) is confidential; (iii) is commercially sensitive; (iv) may not be readily and legally available to others engaged in a similar business to that of the Company or to the general public; and (v) if disclosed, would be liable to cause significant harm to the Company.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{5.2 Definition.} In this Agreement, the term ``Confidential Information'' shall mean and include all information and data relating to the purpose which is obtained, whether in writing, pictorially, in machine readable form, on compact disc, electronic mail, mail or orally in connection with or during the course of the engagement including but without limitation, financial information, schematics, trade secrets, technology, customer lists (potential or actual), Intellectual Property and other customer related information, supplier information, sales statistics, market intelligence, marketing, and other business strategies and other commercial information of a confidential and propriety nature.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{5.3 Obligation of Non-Disclosure and Non-Use.} Unless otherwise agreed by the Company in writing, the Independent Contractor will not use, reveal or disclose the Confidential Information for any purpose whatsoever to any third party except required under any law or under any court order or investigating agency.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{5.4 Period of confidentiality.} The Independent Contractor shall keep secret and shall not at any time either during his engagement with the Company, and {{Confidentiality_Period}} after its expiry or termination, for whatever reason, use, communicate or reveal to any person for his own or another's benefit, any secret or Confidential Information concerning the business, finances or organisation of the Company, its suppliers, customers or clients which shall have come to his knowledge during his engagement with the Company. The Independent Contractor shall also use his best endeavours to prevent the publication or disclosure of any such information.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{5.5 Return of Confidential Information.} During the subsistence of this Agreement or post expiration or termination thereof, the Company may require the Independent Contractor to return any or all the information, original or duplicate, stored in hard drives or any other storage device, including e-drives such as google drive or any similar storage facility immediately to the Company and shall further destroy all said information to safeguard the interest of the Company.

\vspace{1em}
\noindent
\textbf{6. Non-Solicitation and Non-Compete}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{6.1 Non-Competition.} The Independent Contractor hereby irrevocably undertakes that at any time during the subsistence of the engagement with the Company and {{Non_Compete_Period}} thereafter, he shall not carry on or engage in, directly or indirectly, as principal or agent or representative or director or collaborator or consultant or in any other manner whatsoever, whether for profit or otherwise, any business which competes directly or indirectly or remotely with the whole or any pan of the business carried on by the Company or any activity related to the business carried on by the Company, whether by using the Confidential Information or otherwise.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{6.2 Non-Solicitation.} The Consultant agrees that during the validity of this Agreement and for a period of {{Non_Solicitation_Period}} after expiry or termination of this Agreement, he shall not:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item[(i)] divert or attempt to divert from the Company any business of any kind in which it is engaged, including without limitation, solicitation of or interference with any of its suppliers or customers or clients whether individuals or institutions; or
    \item[(ii)] employ, solicit for employment, or recommend for employment any person employed by the Company.
\end{enumerate}

\vspace{1em}
\noindent
\textbf{7. Governing Law and Jurisdiction}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{7.1 Governing Law:} This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{7.2 Jurisdiction.} All disputes arising out of this Agreement shall be settled in accordance with the provisions of Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996 having its seat and venue at {{Jurisdiction}}.

\vspace{1em}
\noindent
\textbf{8. Force Majure.} In the event that either Party is unable to perform any of its obligations under this Agreement as a result of natural disaster, action or decree of government bodies, communication line failures not due to the fault of the affected Party, or any other delay or failure which arises from causes beyond a Party's reasonable control and without negligence or wilful misconduct of the Party otherwise chargeable with such failure, delay or default, the affected Party shall immediately give written notice to the other Party.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the Parties have duly executed this Agreement by their authorised representatives as of the date first written above.
