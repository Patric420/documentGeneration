\noindent
The Company and the Independent Contractor are hereinafter individually referred to as the ``Party'' and collectively as the ``Parties''.

\vspace{1.5em}
\noindent\textbf{WHEREAS:}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
A. The Parties have entered into an Independent Contractor Agreement (the ``ICA'') on \textbf{{{ICA_Date}}}, pursuant whereto the Independent Contractor has agreed to work for the Company in writing, modifying, testing, developing, designing, implementing, customizing, installing, maintaining, upgrading, supporting, and servicing computer programs and software applications to meet the specific needs of particular clients, excluding web-page designing and other ancillary work for a period of {{Term}} from the effective date of ICA.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
B. During the subsistence of the ICA, the Independent Contractor shall be exposed to Confidential information (the ``Confidential information'') about the business of the Company, its clients which (i) amounts to a trade secret; (ii) is confidential; (iii) is commercially sensitive; (iv) may not be readily and legally available to others engaged in a similar business to that of the Company or to the general public; and (v) if disclosed, would be liable to cause significant harm to the Company.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
C. In order to protect the confidentiality of the Confidential Information, the Company has insisted on execution of this Non-Disclosure Agreement.

\vspace{1em}
\noindent
NOW THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and premises of the Parties and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which is hereby acknowledged, the parties agree as follows: -

\vspace{1em}
\noindent
\textbf{1. Confidential Information:}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item[i.] In this Agreement, the term ``Confidential Information'' shall mean and include all information and data relating to the purpose which is obtained, whether in writing, pictorially, in machine readable form, on compact disc, electronic mail, mail or orally in connection with or during the course of the engagement including but without limitation, financial information, schematics, trade secrets, technology, customer lists (potential or actual), Intellectual Property and other customer related information, supplier information, sales statistics, market intelligence, marketing, and other business strategies and other commercial information of a confidential and propriety nature which is not in public domain.
    \item[ii.] During the subsistence of the ICA, the Company shall disclose to the Independent Contractor certain non-public information in any form whether written or oral under this Agreement which is marked as confidential or disclosed under the circumstances indicating its confidentiality.
    \item[iii.] Any information which is disclosed orally shall also be accorded equal confidentiality treatment.
\end{enumerate}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{2. Exclusions:} Confidential Information shall not include the following information
\begin{enumerate}
    \item[i.] Previously known to the Independent Contractor without an obligation of confidence owed to the Company,
    \item[ii.] Independently developed by or for the Independent Contractor without use of or access to Confidential Information, or
    \item[iii.] Acquired by the Independent Contractor from a third-Party which is not known by the Independent Contractor to be under an obligation of confidence owed to the Company with respect to such information, or
    \item[iv.] Which is or has become publicly available through no breach of this Agreement by the Independent Contractor.
\end{enumerate}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{3. Permitted Use and Disclosure:} The Independent Contractor shall:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item[i.] Protect the secrecy of the Confidential Information by using the same degree of care, but no less than a reasonable degree of care, to prevent the unauthorized use, dissemination, or publication of the Confidential Information as the Independent Contractor uses to protect its own Information of a like nature,
    \item[ii.] Use such Confidential Information for exercising its rights and performing its obligations under this Agreement, and
    \item[iii.] Disclose such Confidential Information to any third party, if required, only to the extent necessary in relation to accomplishment of the purpose of this Agreement.
\end{enumerate}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{4. Compelled Disclosure:}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item[i.] To the extent absolutely necessary, the Independent Contractor may disclose Confidential Information, if required by any judicial or governmental request, requirement or order; provided that the Independent Contractor will provide with sufficient prior written notice of such request, requirement or order to the Company so that the Company shall contest such request, requirement or order to oppose the disclosure of Confidential Information.
    \item[ii.] The Independent Contractor shall cooperate with the Company in its attempts to oppose such disclosure.
\end{enumerate}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{5. Ownership:}
\begin{quote}
    The Company is and shall be the sole owner of the Confidential Information developed during the subsistence of the Independent Contractor Agreement.
\end{quote}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{6. Term and Protection Period:}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item[i.] This Agreement shall be in effect for a period of {{Protection_Period}} following the Effective Date or termination whichever is later.
    \item[ii.] The Independent Contractor shall keep secret and shall not at any time either during his engagement with the Company, and 1 (One) year after its expiry or termination whichever is later, for whatever reason, use, communicate or reveal to any person for his own or another's benefit, any secret or Confidential Information concerning the business, finances or organisation of the Company, its suppliers, customers or clients which shall have come to his knowledge during his engagement with the Company. The Independent Contractor shall also use his best endeavours to prevent the publication or disclosure of any such information.
    \item[iii.] During the subsistence of ICA or post expiration or termination thereof, the Company may require the Independent Contractor to return any or all the information, original or duplicate, stored in hard drives or any other storage device, including e-drives such as google drive or any similar storage facility immediately to the Company and shall further destroy all said information to safeguard the interest of the Company.
\end{enumerate}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{7. Representations or Warranties.} The Company represents and warrants to the Independent Contractor that:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item[i.] It has the right to disclose the Confidential Information and shall be liable for any damages suffered by Independent Contractor as a consequence of use of the Confidential Information.
    \item[ii.] It is a company that has been duly incorporated or organized and is validly existing and in good standing.
\end{enumerate}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{8. Indemnity:} The Company shall protect, indemnify, and hold the Independent Contractor harmless from any loss suffered by the Independent Contractor due to the authorized use of the Confidential Information by the Independent Contractor.

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{9. Governing Law and Jurisdiction.}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item[i.] Governing Law: This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.
    \item[ii.] Jurisdiction: All disputes arising out of this Agreement shall be settled in accordance with the provisions of Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996 having its seat and venue at {{Jurisdiction}}.
\end{enumerate}

\vspace{0.5em}
\noindent
\textbf{10. Severability:} If any provision of this Agreement is or has become illegal, invalid or unenforceable in any jurisdiction, the illegality, invalidity or unenforceability of that provision will not affect the validity of any other provision of this Agreement.

\noindent
